About GRAIL

Knowledge, collaboration, and student growth. Our mission is to inspire and promote learning and collaboration between students as they pursue knowledge and understanding.

GRAIL stands for Guided Resource for Academy Inquiry and Learning. Our goal is to make learning easier and more in-depth for students and teachers alike. This new initiative will foster and promote collaboration between students as they grow through their education.

It will also provide students with a better understanding of their learning material, as they are able to ask questions about challenging problems they face, and get detailed, prompt, and in-depth responses.
