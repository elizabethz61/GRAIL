GRAIL Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2025

This platform is designed to encourage respectful academic discussion and peer-to-peer support. By accessing or using this Forum, you agree to the following Terms of Use. Please read them carefully.

  1. Purpose of GRAIL

    This forum is intended for students to:

    • Ask and answer questions about assignments, projects, and more
    • Collaborate on understanding class concepts
    • Support your peers in their learning journey
    • Constructive discussion on course topics
  2. Eligibility

    To use this Forum, you must be:

    • A student enrolled in Middle Georgia State University
    • At least 13 years old
    • Willing to follow these terms and engage respectfully with others.
  3. Community Guidelines

    You are expected to:

    • Be respectful and kind in all interactions.
    • Help others without giving away direct answers to graded assignments (no “answer dropping”).
    • Use clear titles and tags when asking questions.
    • Avoid spam, self-promotion, or unrelated content.

    We do not allow:

    • Sharing complete solutions or answers to homework that may be submitted for a grade.
    • Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
    • Pretending to be someone else or using multiple fake accounts.
  4. Disclaimer

    This Forum is provided “as is.” We make no guarantees about the accuracy, completeness, or usefulness of posts. Always verify answers through your own research or with instructors.

  5. Changes to These Terms

    We may update these Terms occasionally. We'll notify you of major changes, but it's your responsibility to stay informed.

If you have questions or need to report a concern, please contact us at: helpdesk@mga.edu
