GRAIL Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2025

Applies To: All users of GRAIL

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") outlines the rules and guidelines for using GRAIL (the “Platform”). Our mission is to inspire and promote learning and collaboration between students as they pursue knowledge and understanding. By using the Platform, you agree to follow this Policy. Violations may result in content removal, warnings, suspension, or permanent bans.

  1. Academic Integrity

    We are here to learn, not cheat. You may:

    • Ask questions to better understand your assignments
    • Offer explanations, hints, or resources to help others learn

    You may not:

    • Post or request full solutions to assignments meant to be graded
    • Upload answer keys, exam content, or copyrighted course material
    • Misrepresent someone else's work as your own
  2. Respectful Behavior

    Treat others the way you'd like to be treated. You must:

    • Use respectful, inclusive, and non-discriminatory language
    • Constructively criticize ideas—not people
    • Be patient with learners of all levels

    You must not:

    • Harass, bully, or threaten others
    • Use hate speech, slurs, or offensive content
    • Engage in flame wars or intentionally provoke users
  3. Academic Integrity

    By participating, you agree to uphold academic honesty:

    • Do not request or provide full solutions to assignments unless explicitly permitted by your instructor.
    • Do not use this platform to submit or solicit work that will be turned in as your own.
    • Always cite any external resources or sources of help if required by your school or instructor.
  4. Safe and Secure Use

    To keep the platform safe for everyone:

    • Don't share your login credentials or impersonate others
    • Use secure passwords, e.g. using eight characters or more, both upper and lowercase letters, and special characters
    • Report any suspicious activity to helpdesk@mga.edu
  5. Safe and Secure Use

    To keep the platform safe for everyone:

    • Don't share your login credentials or impersonate others
    • Use secure passwords, e.g. using eight characters or more, both upper and lowercase letters, and special characters
    • Report any suspicious activity to helpdesk@mga.edu
  6. Consequences for Misuse

    Violations of these terms may result in:

    • A warning and removal of inappropriate content.
    • Temporary or permanent ban from the platform.
    • Referral to MGA's academic integrity office or disciplinary committee.
  7. Moderation

    Moderators reserve the right to:

    • Remove any posts that violate these terms.
    • Flag or report users for behavior inconsistent with the platform's goals.

Please contact us at helpdesk@mga.edu if you have any questions about this policy or want to appeal an action.
